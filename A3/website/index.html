About Us
  • 01

    Knowledge and Passion, Miguel hand-picks his enourmous range of items, ranging from fine pieces from the area of Paris, to elegant, and sometimes very rustic furniture from Provincial France.

  • 02

    Location, These French antique pieces are displayed mainly in a 'as-found condition' with full restoration and conservation services available at our Malvern premises.

  • 03

    Miguel Meirelles, trained as a conservator of antique furniture at the “Istituto per l’Arte e il Restauro” in Florence (1978-1980) and today runs his Melbourne antiques retail shop at the same premises as his conservation workshop.

  • 04

    Collaboration, Miguel is happy to arrange for short term rentals of his furniture and decorative items to television, film, theatre and advertising studios. Our Melbourne antique shop can organise packaging, insurance and delivery Australia wide, and also for shipping internationally.
